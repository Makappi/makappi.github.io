How to integrate Smile?

  1. FAQ
  2. Integrations
  3. How to integrate Smile?

You can add Smile to your Mobile App your clients will be able to see their points and redeem coupons.

Smile compatible plans

To add Smile to your Mobile App, you need to be at least on the Smile "Growth" Plan.

Add the private key

  • On your Shopify Admin, go to the Smile App
  • Click on "Settings"
  • Click on "Platform"
  • Click on "Show More"
  • Click on "Show" and copy the private key
  • Add this private key to your Integration on Makappi
  • Publish the change to finish the integration

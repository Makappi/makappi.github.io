  2. Updates

When you publish changes from Makappi, most changes are immediately visible in the App. Those changes include the blocks in the home screen, the product page, the collections, themes, icons, menu, etc.

Some modifications require to be submitted to Apple and Google or changes by Makappi support before they can be visible to your users.

Changes on some App assets

There are three updates on the App that are not visible immediately. They are the App name, the App icon, and the Splashscreen image displayed when your App is loading.

Update on the Stores Pages

Changes made to the App Store and the Play Store page are not visible right away. If you update those texts or assets our team will be automatically notified. The changes will be visible to your users after a delay.

Update of some integrations

Enabling some integrations requires a new submission of the App to the App Store (Apple) and the Play Store (Android). Those integrations are Google Analytics, Facebook Analytics, and Google Pay. If your App is live, you have to contact support to enable those integrations.
